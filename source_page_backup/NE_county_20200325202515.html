The data represented in this dashboard is pulled from multiple public sources. These sources update at various times, so information may not always be the most current or accurate. This website should not be used for clinical or medical guidance.   The base layer of the map is pulled from the map created by the Johns Hopkins University Center for Systems Science and Engineering. The number of Confirmed Cases in Nebraska is from multiple sources including the Nebraska Department of Health and Human Services and local public health departments. Population data is from American Community Survey 5-year estimate 2017, for Census Block Groups of Nebraska. Exposure site date is pulled from publicly available reports from News Channel Nebraska.
